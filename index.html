Questions

Good to know before you start.

How quickly can I go live? +

Most shops are live within 24 hours. We build your ordering page and POS on a Local For You sub-domain straight away — and you can connect your own domain whenever you're ready.

Do I need to be technical to use it? +

No. Our team sets it up with you and runs you through it. The whole point of "one screen" is that anyone on your floor can use it without training — and our Thai-speaking support is on live chat 24/7 if you get stuck.

Which delivery apps does it connect to? +

Order aggregation brings Grab, Lineman and ShopeeFood into one screen, alongside your own online, QR and dine-in orders. For your own riders, the POS connects with Uber Direct.

Can I manage more than one branch? +

Yes. Manage every location from a single account, set different staff roles per branch, and check sales across all of them from your phone.

What does online payment cost? +

Online transactions are charged at 1.75% + $0.30 per order, settled directly to you — no separate payment account to manage. Your card and QR payments at the counter reconcile and receipt automatically.

What's included with the hardware package? +

The $299/mo Everything + Hardware plan includes the terminal and a free router extension, on a 24-month contract. The other packages are software-only and month to month.
